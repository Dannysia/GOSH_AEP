What is V.I.S.I.O.N.?

V.I.S.I.O.N. stands for Variable Insight Sourcing Inquiry Operational Network. This system utilizes photos and GPS data from aerial drones to survey damages to power line poles. Data can then be reviewed and labeled for needed maintenance.
